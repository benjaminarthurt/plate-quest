Welcome to Plate Quest!

Everyone plays on one team. See how many different license plates your family can find during the trip.

  1. Keep an eye out for plates from different states, provinces, territories, and special groups.
  2. When your team spots a new one, tap its picture, name, or map area.
  3. Each place can be collected once per trip.
  4. Plates from farther away earn more points.

Road-trip rule: Let a passenger tap the game. Drivers should keep their eyes on the road.
