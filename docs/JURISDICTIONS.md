# Jurisdiction Scope

## Core collections

### United States

All 50 states and the District of Columbia.

### Canada

All ten provinces and three territories:

Alberta, British Columbia, Manitoba, New Brunswick, Newfoundland and Labrador, Northwest Territories, Nova Scotia, Nunavut, Ontario, Prince Edward Island, Quebec, Saskatchewan, and Yukon.

### Mexico

All 31 states and Mexico City.

## United States territories

American Samoa, Guam, Northern Mariana Islands, Puerto Rico, and U.S. Virgin Islands.

## Special plate categories

- U.S. federal government
- Canadian federal government
- Diplomatic
- Consular
- United Nations
- Tribal nation
- Military installation
- Dealer
- Transporter
- Temporary
- Apportioned commercial
- Antique or historic

Special categories without a defensible geographic home use fixed or category-based scoring rather than boundary distance.

## Optional extended North America collection

- Dominican Republic
- Jamaica

- British Virgin Islands
- Saint Pierre and Miquelon
- Greenland

## Plate imagery

Version 1 should use original simplified illustrations rather than copied photographs. Illustrations should communicate major colors, layout, slogan placement, and recognizable design elements without reproducing real serial numbers or security features.
